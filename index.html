Aj-Ajal Online Quran Institute

In the wake of global technological advancement, the international institutions are striving for excellence in the attainment of their set goals. The use of computers is inevitable in all fields of life and it has helped academics a lot. In the current scenario, distance learning has contributed positively in spreading the knowledge and fulfilling the religious needs of the masses using the technology. This educational joint venture has been named as "Al Ajal Online Quran Institute".
